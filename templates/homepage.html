Overview


Everyday, there’s countless emission of carbon dioxide from vehicles that the rider/driver is unaware of. We ourselves are unaware of our ecological foot print. Each vehicle has a different build and accordingly different emission status. There is no real application in the society currently that keeps records the emission of such vehicles. Furthermore, there isn’t a facility for the general public to predict the emission released by their vehicle to get an idea of the pollution caused either.

Thus, keeping all this in mind, this application helps the user to get an estimate of the carbon emission from their vehicle. Also, it helps the user to see the pollution graph history of the vehicle’s manufacturer and suggest alternatives. Simply put it shows the visualization of the datasets and keeps record of them in an offline file. Not only that it also exports the form data of the user to an external online sheet. This app will prove to be useful to not just the general public but the pollution controlling department. Lastly, it will help to promote EVs (electric vehicles).
